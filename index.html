Aircraft Contest

₹150/team

Build an aircraft with given guidelines & materials within 90 minutes

  • Only 3 members per team is allowed
  • The participating teams should build an aircraft with the given guidelines and materials
  • The time limit is 90 minutes
  • The team that takes extra time will get point reductions
  • The model made is evaluated with some criterias explained at the time of the event
  • The team that has the most feasible design wins
